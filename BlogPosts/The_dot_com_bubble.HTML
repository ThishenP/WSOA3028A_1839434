The Dot Com Bubble

The dot com bubble was an extremely important moment in the history of the internet and the way in which internet companies were run. The dot com bubble refers to a period of time in which a massive amount of growth was seen in many internet based companies. This growth led to many people seeing any internet company as a foolproof investment in which nothing could go wrong. People continued to invest large amounts of money in any company that fit the mould but there was a lack of research done into these companies. Full business models were not discussed or developed fully but people invested because of fast growth. The fast growth was mainly due to many buying into the idea of internet business but was not backed by any real financial forecasts or valuations.

In the year 2000 a catastrophe began taking place in which many large internet companies were lost due to the fact that most funding came from hype and misinformation. Once the first few companies started to show bad signs it led to investors getting scared and moving out of the industry in a frenzied fashion. This point in time changed the face of internet companies forever as now they are needed to be more professional and be run more like traditional businesses. This was ultimately a much needed lesson and was important in moving towards a more steady and beneficial economy overall but as we have seen by the crypto currency bubble, history repeats itself. These events show us how important financial information of companies is as well as how important education and regulation of unicorn tech companies is.
