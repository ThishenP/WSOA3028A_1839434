Home

This website is a showcase of myself which highlights my photography and skateboarding career. You will find a portfolio of my photographs and skate videos as well as ideas and thoughts on my blog

Skaterboarding

I am currently a professional skateboarder sponsored by Vans and I am ranked 25th on the official South african skateboarding rankings. On this site I provide advice to beginner skateboarders as well as showcase videos of some of my best tricks. I also provide video tutorials for certain tricks and will take requests for tricks you may want to learn.

Photography

In terms of photography I have a portfolio of my best shots on this site. My photography focuses on the Johannesburg lifestyle and the skate community within Johannesburg. Many of the photos I post fall into the category of urban photography as I am inspired by the architecture and cascading buildings of Johannesburg CBD.
